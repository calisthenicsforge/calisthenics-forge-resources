# Beginner No-Equipment Workout Resource

This is a simple public resource for beginners who want to start calisthenics without equipment.

Calisthenics Forge organizes beginner-friendly no-equipment workout guides by body area, so users can choose a simple starting point based on what they want to train.

The no-equipment section currently includes beginner guides for:

- Back training at home
- Arm workouts without equipment
- Chest workouts using bodyweight exercises
- Bodyweight ab workouts for beginners
- Bodyweight shoulder exercises
- Bodyweight leg exercises at home

These guides are designed for people who want to train at home without dumbbells, machines, or gym access.

For the full collection of beginner-friendly no-equipment workout guides, visit:

https://calisthenicsforge.com/no-equipment/
