# Beginner Bodyweight Ab Workout Routine

This is a beginner-friendly bodyweight ab workout routine designed to train your core at home without equipment.

The guide includes exercises for:

- Upper abs
- Lower abs
- Obliques
- Core stabilization

Common beginner-friendly bodyweight ab exercises include:

- Elbow plank crunches
- Upper abs crunches
- Lower abs leg lift crunches
- Side plank hold
- Dynamic elbow plank crunches
- Abdominal stretch
